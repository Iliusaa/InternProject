## Style Reference

- palette: ink void `#0E0B10`, `#17131A`, `#262129`; dungeon stone `#343038`, `#4B4447`, `#6D6461`; Warrior dark crimson `#6E151D`, `#9E2527`; damage red `#D64A32`; torch/coin gold `#8F5A24`, `#C18432`, `#E0B75C`; sickly green `#35533B`, `#587642`, `#8A9B55`; occult blue-violet `#26364F`, `#415C78`, `#7B6A9B`; bone/parchment `#B8A487`, `#D6C6A1`.

- perspective: MVP art direction is three-quarter top-down. Use `Docs/Visual-Design.md` before future generation prompts.
